    Money and Score

    after serving the guest, you will earn money and points.

    How many?
  • Each guest checkout, you will earn $100 and 100 points.
  • Quickly fulfill guest requests, you will earn bonus points.
  • in 1 second: 80 points; in 3 second: 30 points
  • However, if there is trash in your trolley when serving food to guests, 30 points will be deducted.

    • Try to earn as much money and points as possible!
